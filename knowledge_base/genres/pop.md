# Pop

## Overview
Pop music is designed for mass appeal, prioritizing catchy melodies, repetitive structures, and polished production. It frequently evolves by absorbing elements from other popular genres, resulting in accessible, emotionally resonant tracks.

## Mood and Energy
Pop typically features high energy and upbeat, happy, or romantic moods. Tempos often sit in the danceable range of 100-130 BPM, though power ballads can be much slower.

## Listening Contexts
Pop thrives in social settings, parties, workouts, and mainstream radio. It is versatile, scoring both energetic social events and personal emotional moments.

## Notable Characteristics
Key features include a strong verse-chorus structure, prominent and clear lead vocals, synthesized beats, and highly memorable hooks.
