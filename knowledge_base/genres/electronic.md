# Electronic

## Overview
Electronic music is a broad category produced entirely with synthesizers, drum machines, and software. It focuses on sound design, repetitive grooves, and manipulating audio frequencies to create synthetic soundscapes.

## Mood and Energy
Electronic is heavily energetic and happy, usually featuring very high energy levels. Tempos are rigidly locked to a grid, commonly between 120-130 BPM for danceability.

## Listening Contexts
It is the defining genre for clubs, festivals, and parties. It is also frequently used for intense workouts or gaming.

## Notable Characteristics
Defined by synthesized sounds, a prominent "four-on-the-floor" kick drum pattern, and structures built around tension and release (the "drop").
