# Country

## Overview
Country is an American genre rooted in folk and blues, known for its strong emphasis on storytelling, rural life, and working-class themes. It blends acoustic roots with modern pop and rock production.

## Mood and Energy
Country frequently explores romantic, sad, and happy moods at medium energy levels. The pacing is typically conversational and steady.

## Listening Contexts
It is popular for road trips, outdoor gatherings, and casual social events. Listeners often seek out country for relatable, narrative-driven lyrics.

## Notable Characteristics
Signature elements include acoustic guitars, pedal steel, fiddles, banjos, and a distinct vocal twang with clear, narrative lyricism.
