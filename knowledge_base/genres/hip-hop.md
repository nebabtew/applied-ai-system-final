# Hip-Hop

## Overview
Hip-hop is a rhythm-centric genre built around stylized, rhythmic vocal delivery (rapping) over drum beats and sampled backing tracks. It is a cornerstone of modern urban culture, focusing on lyricism, flow, and heavy bass.

## Mood and Energy
Hip-hop is usually energetic, confident, or angry, operating at medium to high energy levels. Tempos range from slow, heavy trap beats to faster, bouncy rhythms.

## Listening Contexts
Ideal for workouts, parties, driving, and competitive environments. It is a high-impact genre meant to motivate and energize.

## Notable Characteristics
Prominent features include looped drum breaks, heavy sub-bass (808s), complex vocal wordplay, and sample-based production.
