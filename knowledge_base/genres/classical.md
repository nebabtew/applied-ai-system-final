# Classical

## Overview
Classical music encompasses centuries of formal Western musical tradition, characterized by complex orchestration, formal structure, and acoustic instrumentation. It ranges from solo piano pieces to massive symphonic works.

## Mood and Energy
Classical music covers the entire emotional spectrum—from peaceful and melancholy to angry and highly energetic. Energy levels depend entirely on the specific composition and instrumentation.

## Listening Contexts
It is widely used for studying, focused work, meditation, and formal concert listening. It is often sought out for its emotional depth without the distraction of lyrics.

## Notable Characteristics
Distinguishing features include entirely acoustic instruments (strings, woodwinds, brass), complex polyphony, lack of a traditional drum kit, and dramatic shifts in volume (dynamics).
