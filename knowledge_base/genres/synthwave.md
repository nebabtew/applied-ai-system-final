# Synthwave

## Overview
Synthwave is an electronic micro-genre heavily inspired by 1980s film soundtracks, video games, and pop culture. It leverages retro analog synthesizers to create a highly stylized, nostalgic, and futuristic atmosphere.

## Mood and Energy
Synthwave is typically energetic, driving, and melancholy, sitting at medium-high energy. It has a pulsing, persistent tempo that creates a sense of forward motion.

## Listening Contexts
Perfect for night driving, gaming, and coding. It creates an immersive, cinematic backdrop without being overly distracting.

## Notable Characteristics
Characterized by retro analog synth pads, gated reverb snare drums, pulsing 16th-note basslines, and a distinct lack of vocals.
