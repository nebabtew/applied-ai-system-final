# Indie

## Overview
Indie (independent) music prioritizes artistic authenticity and unconventional song structures over mainstream polish. It spans many sub-genres but generally features a DIY aesthetic and emotionally raw delivery.

## Mood and Energy
Indie frequently explores melancholy, sad, and peaceful moods at low to medium energy levels. It leans heavily into introspective and quirky emotional spaces.

## Listening Contexts
Suited for relaxed social hangouts, coffee shops, commuting, and personal downtime.

## Notable Characteristics
Distinguishing features include lo-fi production techniques, unconventional instrumentation, quirky vocal styles, and highly personal or abstract lyrics.
