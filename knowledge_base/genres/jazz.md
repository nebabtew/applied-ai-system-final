# Jazz

## Overview
Jazz is a sophisticated, highly expressive genre built on improvisation, complex harmony, and syncopation. It relies heavily on instrumental interplay and live, unscripted musical conversation between performers.

## Mood and Energy
Jazz can range from energetic and frantic to peaceful and sad, usually operating at medium energy. The tempo and rhythm frequently shift, creating a dynamic emotional landscape.

## Listening Contexts
Jazz is suited for focused listening, sophisticated dinner settings, late-night relaxation, or live lounge environments. 

## Notable Characteristics
Key elements include brass and woodwind instruments (saxophones, trumpets), upright bass, swing rhythms, and extended improvised solos.
