

# Rock

## Overview
Rock is a guitar-driven genre known for its raw power, rebellious spirit, and heavy rhythmic foundation. Originating from blues and country, it spans a massive spectrum from soft acoustic ballads to aggressive, distorted anthems.

## Mood and Energy
Rock spans energetic, angry, and sometimes melancholy moods with medium to very high energy levels. Tempos vary wildly, but the driving rhythm section keeps the energy forward-moving.

## Listening Contexts
Listeners choose rock for workouts, live concerts, driving, or when seeking a cathartic emotional release. It is built for high-volume, immersive listening.

## Notable Characteristics
Distorting electric guitars, powerful drum kits, prominent basslines, and passionate, often gritty vocal performances define the genre.
