# Ambient

## Overview
Ambient music focuses on the textural and atmospheric qualities of sound rather than traditional rhythm or melody. It is designed to evoke a sense of space and environment, blending seamlessly into the background.

## Mood and Energy
Ambient is entirely peaceful and melancholy, with extremely low energy. It often lacks a defined tempo or beat entirely.

## Listening Contexts
Used almost exclusively for sleep, meditation, deep focus, and anxiety relief. It provides atmosphere without demanding the listener's attention.

## Notable Characteristics
Defined by long, sustained synthesizer pads, heavily reverberated sounds, the absence of percussion, and slowly evolving sonic textures.
