# Lofi

## Overview
Lofi (low-fidelity) is a relaxing, atmospheric genre characterized by its intentional use of sonic imperfections like vinyl crackle and tape hiss. It heavily samples jazz and old hip-hop, blending them with simple, repetitive drum loops to create a nostalgic, cozy aesthetic.

## Mood and Energy
Lofi is synonymous with peaceful, melancholy, and focused moods at very low energy levels. Tempos are typically slow to moderate (70-90 BPM), providing a steady, unobtrusive rhythm.

## Listening Contexts
This genre is famously used as background music for studying, coding, or relaxing. It is ideal for solitary, long-duration tasks where listeners want rhythm without distraction.

## Notable Characteristics
Distinguishing features include muffled drum beats, jazz chords, environmental soundscapes (like rain), and a warm, unpolished audio quality.
