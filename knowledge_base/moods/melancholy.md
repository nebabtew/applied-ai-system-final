# Melancholy

## Emotional Quality
Melancholy is a complex, bittersweet emotion. It is not purely sad, but rather a reflective, nostalgic state that finds quiet beauty in longing or gentle regret.

## Sonic Markers
Slower tempos, minor or ambiguous chord progressions, reverb-heavy instrumentation, and subdued, breathy vocals are common. It often features acoustic or lo-fi textures.

## Pairs Well With
Melancholy pairs wonderfully with lofi, indie, synthwave, and classical. It is excellent for rainy days, train rides, and quiet, solitary reflection.n
