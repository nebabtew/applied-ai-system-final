# Intense

## Emotional Quality
Intense music delivers high-stakes emotional or physical energy. It demands engagement rather than offering relaxation, channeling drive, urgency, or focused power. Listeners often turn to intense music to summon motivation or amplify a charged emotional state.

## Sonic Markers
High energy levels, fast tempos (often above 120 BPM), driving percussion, and prominent dynamic contrast define this mood. Low acousticness and high danceability are common.

## Pairs Well With
Intense mood pairs naturally with rock, hip-hop, electronic, and high-energy pop. It suits workouts, competitive contexts, focused performance tasks, and moments requiring full engagement.
