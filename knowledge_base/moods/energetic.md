# Energetic

## Emotional Quality
Energetic music is highly stimulating, driving, and motivating. It creates a sense of urgency, excitement, and physical activation, making it hard to sit still while listening.

## Sonic Markers
Fast tempos, high volumes, heavy percussion, prominent basslines, and dynamic, aggressive vocal or instrumental performances generate this energy.

## Pairs Well With
This mood matches seamlessly with electronic, hip-hop, rock, and pop. It is the go-to mood for workouts, parties, competitive gaming, and running.
