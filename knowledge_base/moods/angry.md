# Angry

## Emotional Quality
Angry music channels frustration, defiance, aggression, and intensity. It offers a safe, cathartic release for heavy, negative emotions and high-tension physical energy.

## Sonic Markers
Distorted instruments, harsh or screaming vocals, aggressive drum beats, and fast tempos are hallmarks. The mix is typically dense, loud, and abrasive.

## Pairs Well With
Angry moods are most closely associated with rock, heavy metal, and aggressive hip-hop. It is commonly used for intense workouts or releasing emotional steam.
