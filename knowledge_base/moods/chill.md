# Chill

## Emotional Quality
Chill music creates a relaxed, low-pressure atmosphere that supports unwinding without demanding attention. It feels easygoing, neither aggressively energetic nor deeply emotional, occupying a calm middle ground that suits background listening or casual focus.

## Sonic Markers
Mid-to-low energy levels, smooth production, gentle percussion, and unhurried melodic phrasing define this mood. Tempos generally fall between 70 and 100 BPM with moderate acoustic content.

## Pairs Well With
Chill mood aligns with lofi, jazz, ambient, and softer electronic subgenres. It fits coffee shops, casual study sessions, weekend mornings, and low-key social settings.
