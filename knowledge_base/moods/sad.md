# Sad

## Emotional Quality
Sad music channels grief, loss, heartbreak, and longing. It provides a space for emotional release and processing, letting listeners sit with sorrow without trying to escape it. The mood is heavy, introspective, and often cathartic.

## Sonic Markers
Slow tempos (50–80 BPM), minor keys, sparse arrangements, and low valence define sad music. Vocals are often raw or subdued. Dynamics tend to be quiet with occasional swells. High acousticness is common but not required.

## Pairs Well With
Sad mood pairs well with R&B, indie, classical, and jazz. It suits late-night reflection, breakups, grief, rainy days, and solo listening sessions where emotional depth is welcome.
