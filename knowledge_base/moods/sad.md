# Relaxed

## Emotional Quality
Relaxed music supports calm presence and gentle ease without slipping into sleepiness. It conveys a sense of unhurried comfort, suiting moments of quiet enjoyment, conversation, or light activity. The mood feels warm rather than emotionally heavy.

## Sonic Markers
Low-to-mid energy, gentle tempos (70–100 BPM), warm acoustic textures, and smooth instrumentation define this mood. High acousticness and moderate valence are common.

## Pairs Well With
Relaxed mood pairs well with jazz, lofi, acoustic indie, and soft classical. It fits dinners, slow afternoons, casual reading, and unhurried social gatherings.
