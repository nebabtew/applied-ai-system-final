# Happy

## Emotional Quality
Happy music promotes joy, optimism, celebration, and lightheartedness. It is instantly uplifting and designed to boost mood and encourage a positive outlook.

## Sonic Markers
Major keys, upbeat tempos, bright instrumentation (like acoustic guitars or brass), and catchy, sing-along vocal hooks create a happy sonic environment.

## Pairs Well With
Happy moods align strongly with pop, electronic, and upbeat country. It is perfect for parties, sunny days, celebrations, and lifting spirits.
